Reverse Dalgona Iced Coffee

Drinks

Prep time: 5 mins

Servings: 1

This whipped coffee is made in the same glass that it is served in.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee granules
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1/2 cup milk

Directions

  1. Place coffee, sugar, and water, in that order, in a tall glass. Using a hand-held milk frother, froth mixture until light and fluffy, 1 to 3 minutes.
  2. Add ice cubes to the glass, then pour milk over top.
